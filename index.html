Our Story

A Half-Century of Fighting for Injury Victims

Founded by Philip M. Gerson in 1970, Gerson & Schwartz has spent over fifty years as one of Miami's most respected personal injury firms. We have appeared in every Florida state and federal court, and we don't back down — we bring cases to trial when that's what justice requires.

Contrary to the trend with many larger firms, we have remained deliberately small. Every client's case is worked on by our partners directly. You will never be handed off to a junior associate or lost in a system built for volume. Your case matters here.

Our senior partners are honors graduates of the University of Miami School of Law and hold the highest attorney rating — AV Preeminent® — awarded by Martindale-Hubbell. They are consistently recognized in "Super Lawyers" and South Florida Business Review's "Top Lawyers."

"Our mission is simple. Our job is to find the truth and use it in courtrooms to get justice for our clients and thereby promote safety for others. We won't hesitate to bring your case to trial — to a jury, and to the highest court in the land."

— Gerson & Schwartz, Accident & Injury Lawyers
